is a giant raccoon and the companion of the master enka music ninja, Sabu. 

 Personality 
Ponta will carelessly attack any enemy head on.Naruto chapter 469, page 1

 Appearance 
Ponta is very large in terms of size, easily towering over his partner Sabu, and was even mistaken for a bear by Kisame Hoshigaki.Naruto chapter 470, page 10 He predominantly has orange fur with some brown fur around his face, ears, feet and tail. The area directly around his mouth is white however. He also wears bandages on his front paws and has small black eyes.

 Part II 
 Five Kage Summit 
During Killer B's enka training with Sabu, Ponta alerted the two of Kisame's presence. Wasting no time, Ponta rushed in to attack Kisame, but was quickly defeated. As the battle between the two parties waged on, Ponta was caught in Kisame's Water Release: Great Exploding Water Colliding Wave technique. However, Killer B lured Kisame — who was fused with Samehada — away from Ponta and Sabu, freeing them from the moving water dome.

 Trivia 

 Ponta was salvaged from an unused concept of a bear-like pet raccoon named . Pochi, together with a character called , was originally planned to appear instead of Haku and Zabuza Momochi. Instead of Zabuza's Kubikiribōchō, Kintarō wielded a giant axe that was also salvaged for Ponta's partner, Sabu.
 Ponta is written as "ポン太". This name is a stereotypical name to give to pet tanuki, who are visually very similar to raccoons, a bit like "Fido" is for a dog or "Mittens" for a cat. It is also the name Saburō Kitajima used when he was part of the comical duo .
