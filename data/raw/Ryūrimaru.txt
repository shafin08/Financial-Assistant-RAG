was Hisen's summoning creature. 

 Background 
Ryūrimaru was summoned by Shibuki in his battle against Suien.

 Personality 
Ryūrimaru was obedient and loyal towards its summoner, Hisen.

 Appearance 
Ryūrimaru was a crimson white koi fish that was six times the size of a human man. 

 Abilities 
Ryūrimaru had the ability to use the Transformation Technique. With Hisen saying, "Dragon God Transform", it transforms into a giant golden dragon.
