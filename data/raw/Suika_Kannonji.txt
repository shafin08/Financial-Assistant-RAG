is a kunoichi of Konohagakure who works at the Mission Assignment Desk.

 Appearance 
Suika has shoulder length pink hair and black pupiless eyes with brown eyebrows. She has ribbons shaped into a flower on the left side of her head. She wears a lavender blouse with puffed sleeves and a violet skirt.

 New Era 
 Genin Mission Arc 

Suika appears in this arc.

 Versus Momoshiki Arc 

Suika appears in this arc.

 Jūgo Arc 

Suika appears in this arc.

 Chūnin Re-Examination Arc 

Suika paid Kawaki his earnings from his completed missions.

 Great Sea Battle of Kirigakure Arc 

Suika briefed Inojin and Chōchō on Jingo Kumano, a missing-nin from Kumo.

 Trivia 
 While she has not appeared in the manga, Suika has been mentioned in supplemental material in Boruto volume 9.
