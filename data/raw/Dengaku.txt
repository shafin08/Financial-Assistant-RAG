Title: Dengaku
URL: https://naruto.fandom.com/wiki/Dengaku
-----------------------

owns a small oden stall in Tanzaku Quarters. Jiraiya and Tsunade frequently go there for drinks while they are in town.

 Appearance 
Dengaku has a short crop of black hair and sideburns that form into a full beard. He also has small dark-coloured eyes.

 Part I 
 Search for Tsunade 
Dengaku was seen attending his oden stall at Tanzaku Quaters.
