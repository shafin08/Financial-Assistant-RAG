Title: Takishi
URL: https://naruto.fandom.com/wiki/Takishi
-----------------------

was a prisoner at Orochimaru's Northern Hideout.

 Personality 
Takishi was very arrogant and hated to lose. Even when fatally injured, he would continue to mock his enemies.

 Appearance 
Takishi wore the typical attire of all Orochimaru's prisoners which consisted of a white T-shirt and a matching pair of pants. He also had short, messy, light-coloured (black in the anime) hair and black eyes.

 Part II 
 Itachi Pursuit Mission 
After being defeated by Hebi, he mockingly warned Suigetsu Hōzuki of the dangers involved in releasing Jūgo. Even though Suigetsu claimed that they could handle him, Takishi continued to warn him of their impending doom, prompting the latter to kill him.
