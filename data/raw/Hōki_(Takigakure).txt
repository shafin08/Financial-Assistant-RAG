 Appearance 
Hoki has brown curly hair (spiky in the manga) and black eyes. He wears a grey shirt over a blue jacket. He also wears a blue Takigakure forehead protector.

 Part I 
 Chūnin Exams 

Hoki was a genin who took part in the Chūnin Exams. After listening to Naruto's speech, he passed with flying colours. He would later run out of strength and fail in the Forest of Death.
