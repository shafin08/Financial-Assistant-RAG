is a citizen from Takigakure.

Personality
Himatsu has been shown to have great faith in Shibuki no matter what happens.

Appearance
Himatsu has a large crop of spiky black hair and big black eyes. He wears a blue long-sleeved jacket and tannish-coloured pants.

 Plot Overview 
He and his sister, Shizuku, are kidnapped by Suien to force Shibuki to give up the Hero Water. Despite Shibuki's well-known cowardice, Himatsu and Shizuku do not lose faith in him and hold out until they are saved by Naruto and Sasuke.
