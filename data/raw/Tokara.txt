Title: Tokara

is a genin of Konohagakure.

 Background  
During the Chūnin Exams, Tokara and his teammates, Hayate and Ibiki, fought against Team Orochimaru in the second stage.Naruto chapter 599, page 4

 Appearance 
Tokara had short brown hair and black eyes. He wore a simple black outfit along with arm guards and his bandanna-styled forehead protector. He also carried a large gourd strapped to his back.
