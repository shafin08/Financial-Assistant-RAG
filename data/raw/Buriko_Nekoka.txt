Title: Buriko Nekoka

-----------------------

is a genin of Konohagakure.

 Personality 
She seems to enjoy watching battles.

 Appearance 
Buriko is a fair-skinned girl with blue eyes and brown hair. She wears a purple cloak with a hood draped over her head.

 New Era 
 Ao Arc 

At the Training Hall, Buriko watched Boruto Uzumaki fight the Seventh Hokage alongside other genin.

Trivia
 Buriko's full name roughly translates to "childish feline".
 Buriko is a fanmade original character that won the Boruto: Naruto Next Generations Chapter #7 Borukura original character contest, created by Cham Neko of Osaka. She appeared alongside other winning entries in Chapter #16.
