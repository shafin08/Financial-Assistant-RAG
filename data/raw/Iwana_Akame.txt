is a chūnin of Konohagakure.

 Background 
In the past, Iwana participated in a foreign espionage mission, where he was attacked and lost his left eye. In the anime, he and Bekkō were seen alongside Daikoku Funeno at the Academy, watching Itachi Uchiha get attacked by bullies.Naruto Shippūden episode 452

 Personality 
Iwana loathes Naruto Uzumaki and thinks that he should be captured and punished for stealing the Scroll of Seals. In the anime, he showed himself as a teacher who worried about the students, who immediately rushed to separate the fighting.

 Appearance 
Iwana's attire is that of the standard Konoha shinobi complete with a flak jacket and a forehead protector which he wears like a bandanna. He also wears an eye-patch over his left eye.

 Part I 
 Prologue — Land of Waves 

Iwana was one of the many shinobi who gathered to track down Naruto after he stole the Scroll of Seals from the Hokage's residence.
