Title: Hikaku Uchiha
URL: https://naruto.fandom.com/wiki/Hikaku_Uchiha
-----------------------

was a member of the Uchiha clan before the founding of Konohagakure. 

 Appearance 
He had shoulder-length brown hair that framed his face and the rest of which he tied into a high ponytail. He wore a high-collared purple shirt that had a chain of sorts draped around the right shoulder.

 Abilities 
Despite his young age, he was one of the clan's most powerful shinobi and was also endowed with great leadership skills. He possessed the clan's kekkei genkai, the Sharingan.
