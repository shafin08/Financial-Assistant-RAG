is a shinobi of Konohagakure and a member of the Analysis Team in the village's Intelligence Division.

 Personality 
While much is not known of his personality, Mawashi is renowned as the "Demon Sergeant of the Analysis Team" because of his hard leadership style.

 Appearance 
Mawashi is a bespectacled man with short cropped brown hair. He wears the standard attire of the Konoha Torture and Interrogation Force, which comprises of a grey suit with a black belt and shinobi sandals.

 Abilities 

Not much has been seen in the way of Mawashi's abilities but he is skilled enough to aid Inoichi in delving into the minds of the enemy in order to retrieve potentially valuable information.

 Part II 
 Pain's Assault 

Mawashi, along with his junior Tonbo Tobitake, and Shimon Hijiri facilitated in Inoichi Yamanaka's Psycho Mind Transmission of Yūdachi. When Pain's assault on Konoha began, they helped to distract the Animal Path in order to give Inoichi a chance to analyse what he's learned.

 Trivia 
 His name comes from . "Dokuraku" is an alternate reading of 独楽 and "Mawashi" is the i-form of 回す.
