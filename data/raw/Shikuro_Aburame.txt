was a shinobi from Konohagakure's Aburame clan.

 Background 
Shikuro was one of the few Aburame to use the rare rinkaichū. Tobi claims Shikuro and his kin had the antidote to their poison as well as having the antibodies in their blood to make them immune to their poison.Naruto chapter 475, page 8 His son, Torune, was known to possess these insects as well. In the anime, it was revealed that Shikuro died while his son was still young, leading Torune to be raised by Shibi Aburame.Naruto: Shippūden episode 317

 Abilities 
Like all other Aburame, Shikuro's body was used as a host to house insects. However, unlike most other Aburame who use kikaichū, Shikuro had rinkaichū which, instead of consuming an opponent's chakra were used to destroy their cells with poison.

 Trivia 
 Shikuro's name may be a corruption of the Japanese romanization of the word cyclone, , similar to his son and Tatsuma, who may be named after tornadoes, and fellow clansman Harika after hurricanes.
