Title: Mizugoke
URL: https://naruto.fandom.com/wiki/Mizugoke
-----------------------

is a mercenary ninja. 

 Appearance 
Mizugoke has fair skin, short black hair and thin, black eyebrows, wears a light-grey tightsuit (under it is white bandages all over his body), and wears black ninja sandals.

 Abilities 
Mizugoke has good chakra control. 

 Plot Overview 
Mizugoke had snuck into Konoha and stolen important documents. He encountered Neji and Naruto when they defeated his brother. 

 Trivia 
 The kanji used to write his name mean "peat moss".
