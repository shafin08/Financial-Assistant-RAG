Rainenmaru was one of Suien's summons who appeared in Naruto: Mission: Protect The Waterfall Village! (novel). 

 Personality 
Rainenmaru was very loyal to Suien. He was also violent towards his opponents. 

 Appearance 
Rainenmaru was a giant black catfish.

 Abilities 
Rainenmaru had incredible speed and was powerful enough to take down Ryūrimaru and Naruto Uzumaki with a single blow.
