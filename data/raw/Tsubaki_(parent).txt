Title: Tsubaki (parent)
URL: https://naruto.fandom.com/wiki/Tsubaki_(parent)
-----------------------

is the mother of one of the students in Naruto's class at the Ninja Academy.

 Appearance 
Tsubaki has long brown hair, with her bangs hanging over her forehead. She also has brown eyes. She wears a long-sleeved white shirt with a black tank-top like shirt over it.

 Personality 
Tsubaki seems to hate Naruto and the fact that he has the Nine-Tails inside of him.

 Part I 
 Prologue — Land of Waves 

Tsubaki was glad to see that Naruto, the jinchūriki of the Nine-Tailed Demon Fox, did not graduate with the rest of his class.
