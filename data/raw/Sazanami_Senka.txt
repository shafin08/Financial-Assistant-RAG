is a genin from Kirigakure.

 Appearance 
Like his brothers, Sazanami sports blue hair and a standard, striped light blue Kirigakure uniform, with a sword attached to his back.

 Abilities 
Sazanami is able to produce two shadow clones.

 New Era 
 Versus Momoshiki Arc 

Having travelled to Konohagakure to participate in the Chūnin Exams, Sazanami alongside his brothers raced towards the exam venue alongside the other participants of the exams before getting eliminated. Listening to his older brother, the triplets bypassed all obstacles on their route. After arriving at Exam Venue B, the team participated in a true or false question, which resulted in all competitors falling into a pit which contained a pool of ink. Not falling into the ink, the team passed the first round of the Chūnin Exams.

Three days later, for the second round of the exams, Sazanami's team competed in a game of capture the flag against Team Konohamaru. After their team protected their flag using genjutsu, the brothers approached the enemies flag which was guarded by Boruto Uzumaki and his Shadow Clones. Defeating the clones, the three attempted to grab the flag, but are stopped and defeated by Boruto's elemental attacks, during which Sarada Uchiha captured their flag, resulting in his team being disqualified.
