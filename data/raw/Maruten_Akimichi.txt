is a tokubetsu jōnin of Konohagakure's Akimichi clan.

 Background 
In the anime, it was shown that Maruten entered the Academy at the same time as Itachi Uchiha. The two also attended the same class.Naruto Shippūden episode 452

 Personality 
Maruten adores Shitō Akimichi like a brother and even attempts to copy his hairstyle and face paint.

 Appearance 
Like other members of the Akimichi clan he is very robust, also, like most members of his clan he wears a samurai-like outfit with the kanji for  on the front and has distinctive circular markings on his cheeks and chin. He wears his short light coloured hair in a topknot ponytail style. He also carries a bō.

As a child, he wore a brown hat, an orange-white shirt and overalls with the kanji for  on them, akin to the outfit he wears in his adulthood.

 Abilities 
Although the full extent of his abilities are unknown, as an Akimichi, Maruten can convert calories into chakra in order to perform his clan's secret techniques. He was also seemingly proficient with wielding a bō.

 Part II 
 Fourth Shinobi World War: Confrontation 

During the Fourth Shinobi World War, he was sent to the Island Turtle alongside several other Konohagakure shinobi, to ensure that Naruto Uzumaki remained within the confines of his training room.Naruto chapter 534, page 16 As he and his clansman moved in to stop Naruto from leaving the falls, they, along with several of the other shinobi were sent flying backward through the falls by Naruto. They are later ordered by Shibi to follow Naruto when he escaped.

 Kaguya Ōtsutsuki Strikes 

In the anime, Maruten is seen after being freed from the Infinite Tsukuyomi by Naruto and Sasuke.
