Title: Tora (Anbu)
URL: https://naruto.fandom.com/wiki/Tora_(Anbu)
-----------------------

was a member of the Anbu, working directly under the supervision of the Second Hokage. 

 Background 
He was one of the Anbu ordered to watch the Uchiha clan to keep them from rebelling again. 

 Appearance 
Tora wears a black Anbu cloak with what appears to be cat-like ear spots on the top of it and a white cat-like Anbu mask.

 Abilities 
He was said to be very skilled. Endowed with tiger-like strength and walking ability; any target he aimed for had no hopes of escape.

 Trivia 
  means "tiger". This is likely a reference to him being said to have tiger-like abilities.
 Tora is likely not his real name, but a codename.
