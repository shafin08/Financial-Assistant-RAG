 New Era 
 Academy Entrance Arc 

Upon enrolling into the Academy, Kuwa was placed in Shino Aburame's homeroom.

Kuwa and his class went to Kirigakure for a field trip, where they were greeted by their tour guide, Kagura Karatachi.

 Graduation Exams Arc 

When Shino's class had the opportunity to become shinobi, Kuwa chose not to participate in the Genin Exams.
