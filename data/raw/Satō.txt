is a genin of Konohagakure, forming a team with Shio and Koshō.

 New Era 
 Ao Arc 

At the Training Hall, Satō watched Boruto Uzumaki fight the Seventh Hokage alongside other genin.

Trivia
 Satō's name translates to "sugar", while her teammates' names translate to "salt" and "pepper", respectively.
 Satō and her team are fanmade original characters that won the Boruto: Naruto Next Generations Chapter #11 Borukura original character contest, created by Haruharu of Ibaraki Prefecture. She appeared alongside other winning entries in Chapter #16.
