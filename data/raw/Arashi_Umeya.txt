Title: Arashi Umeya
URL: https://naruto.fandom.com/wiki/Arashi_Umeya
-----------------------

is a shinobi from Sunagakure.

 Appearance 
Arashi is a tall young man with spiky brown hair that he keeps under a turban. His outfit consists of a long black cape over grey pants and dark brown vest, and a face mask. He also carries his weapon strapped to his back.

 Abilities 
Not much is known of Arashi's abilities other than that he fights using a scythe that is connected to metal spikes with what appears to be electrical wires. Through those metal spikes, Arashi is able to generate lightning.

 In Other Media 
 OVAs 
 Chūnin Exam on Fire! Naruto vs. Konohamaru 

Arashi made it to the final stages of the Chūnin Exams, however, it is unknown who he fought during the exam, and what the outcome of that fight was.
