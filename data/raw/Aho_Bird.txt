Title: Aho Bird
URL: https://naruto.fandom.com/wiki/Aho_Bird
-----------------------

This bird usually appears in the Naruto anime after Naruto and his comrades make an embarrassing blunder and would fly over-head in an awkward yet comical fashion shouting  continuously, adding insult to injury.

 Appearance 
The aho bird is small with a thin black feathered body. It has large, dark-coloured eyes and a dark grey beak. It has two tiny stubby legs and two small wings.

 Trivia 

  is a Japanese pun; it is a Japanese onomatopoeia for a crow's call. It is often used in comedic situations in other anime.
 This bird has also appeared in numerous episodes of the Naruto spin-off, Naruto SD: Rock Lee no Seishun Full-Power Ninden.
 The Aho Bird appears in various other series serving the same function.
