Title: Mukade
URL: https://naruto.fandom.com/wiki/Mukade
-----------------------

was a jōnin from Sunagakure who left Suna and became a servant of Akatsuki member Sasori. 

 Appearance 
Mukade had short spiky brown hair. He wore an Akatsuki uniform when pretending to be Kisame.

 Abilities 
Although his full potential was never shown, it is said that it was his talent that made him worthy to play Kisame to stop Team Guy.Third Databook, page 187

 Part II 
 Kazekage Rescue Mission 

He was used as a sacrifice and was transformed into Kisame Hoshigaki as a means to stall Team Guy while the Akatsuki extracted the One-Tailed Shukaku from Gaara. Might Guy killed the Kisame clone (and in turn Mukade) with his Morning Peacock. His corpse was later devoured by Zetsu.
