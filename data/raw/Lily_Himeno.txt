 Appearance 
Lily is described as having lovely facial features and blonde shoulder length hair that's styled in loose waves. She wears white boots and a mini-dress with ribbons and frills sewed onto it. Her hair is revealed as a wig. Her true appearance is short reddish brown hair and she wears a purple uniform. After no longer being with the Purple Moon Island cult, she starts wearing jeans and relaxed clothing. 

 Abilities 
Despite not being trained as a kunoichi, Lily was taught to harness her innate talent for Lightning Release. Having trained constantly for five years, since her youth, she has the ability to generate powerful electric currents. Similar to Magnet Release, doing so produces a large electromagnetic field that allows her to freely manipulate metal objects, like kunai.Sasuke Shinden: The Teacher's Star Pupil page 81 Lily is able to fine tune her Lighting Release to send bioelectric signals into a target in order to completely manipulate their body.
