is a ninja from Konohagakure.

 Background 
An entry seen briefly in a bingo book prior to Chiriku and Asuma Sarutobi's in the anime showed that Kiyomu has a 7.8 billion ryō bounty on their head. 

 Trivia 
 Kiyomu Fukuda was named after the director and storyboard artist for episode 76.
